Chichicastenango Market

One of the largest and most well-known markets in all of Central America can be found in the small indigenous Mayan town of Chichicastenango, located in the western highlands of Guatemala.

Chichicastenango Market

The market is held every Thursday and Sunday and this is when locals travel from villages throughout the countryside and gather at this market to sell their beautiful handicrafts, colourful textiles, fresh produce, traditional clothing and more at this bustling and vibrant local market.
