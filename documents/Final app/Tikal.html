Temple I – The Great Jaguar Temple

Temple I – the Great Jaguar temple – was built for the 26th ruler of Tikal, Jasaw Chan K'awiil; whether it was his burial tomb, or used during his life, is uncertain.

: THE TALUD-TABLERO TEMPLE (5C-49)

Structure 5C-49 within the Lost World complex is also know as the Talud-Tablero Temple thanks to its architectural style. The “Talud” is a steeply sloping wall, whilst the “Tablero” is a table-like right-angled protrusion.
